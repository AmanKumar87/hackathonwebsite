Official Languages

India has 22 officially recognized languages, including:

  • Hindi
  • English
  • Bengali
  • Telugu
  • Marathi

Regional Languages

In addition to the official languages, India has many regional languages spoken in different states and union territories. Some of the prominent regional languages include:

  • Tamil
  • Urdu
  • Kannada
  • Gujarati
  • Punjabi
